Artists Meet the Metaverse


Welcome to the metaverse, human!
Before starting, you may want to check the information section
to understand better what is a cryptoparty (it's cool heheh).


We are building the first hicetnunc cryptoparty festival experience
based on the VR web, so everyone in the world can join us.
The possibilities here are endless and we are calling for people
to make part of it. It doesn't matter who you are and what you do,
there will always be somewhere you can be important.


artists
- to design cool promos, poster, gifs
- to expose their works during the event
- to present a workshop on a virtual stand during the event
- to make textures for objects / sky / installations / lights
- 3D artists to sculpt objects and ambients


musicians, producers and DJs
- to play live during the event lineup

software magicians
- who can make code art
- who can deal with basic web technologies such as HTML, CSS and JS
- who understand about node servers and multiplayer
- who have basic understandings on A-Frame (ultra simple html lib for VR)

others
- if you want to present a workshop on any subject
- organization and production
- marketing
- if you have cool ideas

We'd be pleased to welcome you on our cryptoparty discord server for further debates and organization!
